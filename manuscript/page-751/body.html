If I had chosen to be an actor, I should have been the greatest actor living! No disguises—no grease paint—no false beards! Personality! I put it on like a glove! When I shed it, I was myself, quiet, unobtrusive, a man like every other man. I called myself Mr. Brown. There are hundreds of men called Brown—there are hundreds of men looking just like me....

“... I succeeded in my false career. I was bound to succeed. I shall succeed in the other. A man like me cannot fail....

“... I have been reading a life of Napoleon. He and I have much in common....

“... I make a practice of defending criminals. A man should look after his own people....
